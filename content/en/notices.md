## Notices

sDB displays search results based on public materials published by MEXT, including school information, school codes, and institution-type materials.

## Processing

Institution names, faculty or graduate-school names, department names, prefectures, and institution types are extracted and processed for search. Readings, romanized search terms, academic fields, academic tracks, and prefecture codes may also be added.

Some public materials have limited granularity, especially for high schools and vocational schools. Classifications and supplements may be mechanical or heuristic, and do not guarantee official department offerings for each institution.
