## Overview

sDB is a page for searching Japanese educational institutions.

You can narrow candidates by institution type, prefecture, institution name, faculty or graduate school, and department.

## How To Use

1. Select an institution type and prefecture as needed.
2. Enter an institution name in Japanese, hiragana, katakana, roman letters, English, or an abbreviation.
3. Select an institution from the candidates.
4. Select a faculty, graduate school, or department.

## Search Scope

Search targets include universities, graduate schools, junior colleges, colleges of technology, high schools, and vocational schools.

Search results may include information processed from public materials published by MEXT. For important decisions, always confirm the latest official source.
